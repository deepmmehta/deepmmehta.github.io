🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026  ·  Effective: 20 June 2026

Summary: Gifter stores all your personal data (names, photos, gift history) only on your device. We do not sell or share your personal information. Data that leaves your device is limited to: (1) anonymised quiz queries sent to generate AI suggestions, (2) anonymous crash reports sent to Sentry (no personal details), and (3) anonymous usage analytics sent to PostHog (screens visited, features used — no names or personal data).

1. Who we are

Gifter is an AI-powered gift idea companion built for the Indian market, developed and operated by Deep Mehta ("we", "us", "our"). For any privacy-related questions, contact us at gifterappindia@gmail.com.

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Gifter collects, how it is used, how it is protected, and your rights over it. It applies to the Gifter mobile application available on Android (Google Play Store) and to any related services we operate in connection with the App.

By downloading, installing, or using Gifter, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the data practices described herein. If you do not agree with any part of it, please do not use the App.

3. Information we collect

3a. Information you provide directly

  • People profiles — names, relationships, birthdays, anniversaries, notes, and profile photos you add to "My People".
  • Gift history — occasions, gift ideas, costs, and reactions you log.
  • Wishlists — items you add to "My Wishlist" or to a saved person's wishlist.
  • Custom events & reminders — event labels, dates, and emoji you add in the Upcoming screen.
  • Group gift entries — gift names, participant counts, and payment tracking you record.
  • Quiz answers — your responses to the gift-finding questionnaire (relationship type, occasion, budget, preferences). These are transient — not stored after the AI response is returned.
  • Voice input — if you choose to use the microphone for voice typing during a quiz question, audio is processed directly by your device's system keyboard or speech engine. The App does not record, transmit, or store audio data itself.

3b. Information generated automatically

  • App preferences — onboarding completion status, dark mode setting, notification permissions, contacts-import flag, consent record.
  • Invite counter — a local count of how many times you have used the referral share feature (stored only on device).
  • Device information — OS name and version, used solely for rendering the app correctly. Not transmitted off-device.

3c. Information we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number (there is no account or sign-in).
  • Precise or approximate location data.
  • Payment card or financial information of any kind.
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID) or cross-app tracking identifiers.
  • Browser history, search history, or data from other apps.
  • Audio recordings — the App requests microphone access only to enable system-level voice keyboard input; it does not record or process audio independently.
  • Any data about minors — the App is intended for users aged 18 and above.

4. Where your data is stored

All personal data you enter into Gifter is stored locally on your device only, using your device's secure sandboxed local storage (AsyncStorage, provided by React Native / Expo). We do not operate or control any database, cloud server, or backend that holds your personal data.

This means:

  • We cannot access your data remotely — not even if compelled by a court or government authority.
  • If you delete the App, all data is permanently and irrecoverably deleted from your device.
  • If your device is lost, stolen, or wiped, your Gifter data is lost unless you have exported a backup.

You can export a backup of your data at any time via Settings → Export my data, and restore it on a new device via Settings → Restore from backup.

5. Third-party services

5a. AI gift suggestions (Anthropic / Gifter Proxy)

When you request gift ideas, the App sends the following information to our Cloudflare Worker proxy, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API:

  • Recipient type (e.g. "Friend", "Parent") — never a name
  • Occasion and approximate budget range
  • General preferences selected in the quiz

No names, photos, contact details, birthdates, personal notes, or personally identifiable information are ever sent to Anthropic or our proxy. The query is designed to be fully de-identified.

Anthropic may retain API request logs per its own data policies. See: anthropic.com/privacy
Cloudflare privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

5b. Amazon Associates — affiliate product data

The App fetches product information via the Amazon Product Advertising API (PAAPI) to display deal prices and match gift ideas with Amazon listings. This API call does not include any personal information about you.

Gifter participates in the Amazon Associates Programme (India). If you click an Amazon link in the App and make a purchase, we may earn a small affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. Amazon's own privacy policy governs your activity on Amazon.in. See: Amazon India Privacy Notice

5c. EarnKaro — affiliate links for non-Amazon shops

For "Where to buy" links to shops other than Amazon (e.g. Nykaa, Myntra, Flipkart, etc.), the App routes links through EarnKaro's affiliate conversion service. This converts product links into affiliate links so Gifter may earn a commission if you make a purchase. No personal information about you is shared with EarnKaro — only the destination product URL is processed. See: EarnKaro Privacy Policy

Affiliate disclosure: Gifter earns a small commission from qualifying purchases made through Amazon Associates and EarnKaro links. This commission comes at no extra cost to you and helps keep the App free.

5d. Sentry — crash reporting

Gifter uses Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) to capture anonymous crash reports and error logs. If the app crashes or encounters an unexpected error, a report is sent to Sentry's servers in the United States containing: the error message, an anonymised stack trace, app version, device OS version, and device model. No names, photos, gift history, or other personal data you have entered into the App are included in crash reports. Crash reporting is activated only after you give consent on the consent screen. See: Sentry Privacy Policy

5e. PostHog — usage analytics

Gifter uses PostHog (PostHog, Inc.) to collect anonymous usage analytics. This helps us understand which features are used most and where the App can be improved. The data collected includes: which screens you visit, whether you complete a gift quiz, whether you tap a buy link (platform: Amazon or other), and whether you save a person or mark a gift as bought. No names, ages, photos, relationship details, or personally identifiable information are included in any analytics event. Analytics are activated only after you give consent on the consent screen. PostHog operates in the United States. See: PostHog Privacy Policy

5f. Expo notifications

Gifter uses Expo's push notification infrastructure to deliver birthday and festival reminders. A device-level push token is generated and stored locally. This token may be transmitted to Expo's servers to route the notification to your device. We do not use push tokens for any other purpose. See: expo.dev/privacy

5e. Calendar and contacts (optional, on-device only)

If you choose to "Import birthdays from contacts", the App requests read access to your device contacts solely to extract name and birthday fields. If you choose to import events from your calendar, the App reads calendar events to extract names and dates. This information is copied into the App's local storage and is never transmitted to any server. You may revoke contacts or calendar access at any time in your device Settings.

6. How we use your information

  • To display your saved people, gift history, wishlists, and custom events within the App.
  • To generate personalised AI gift suggestions based on the anonymised quiz inputs you provide.
  • To schedule and deliver birthday and festival reminder notifications you have opted into.
  • To allow you to export and restore your data across devices.
  • To surface relevant product deals from Amazon via affiliate API calls.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not use your data for any purpose other than operating the App on your device.

7. Sharing of your information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties except as described in Section 5 (anonymised AI queries, push notification routing, and anonymous affiliate link conversion). We do not disclose your data to:

  • Marketing or advertising companies
  • Data brokers or analytics firms
  • Social media platforms
  • Any other third party not described in this policy

If you use the App's Share features (sharing a wishlist, gift ideas, or group gift message), the content you choose to share is sent via your device's native sharing functionality directly to the recipient app of your choice (e.g. WhatsApp). We do not intercept, store, or process that content.

8. Your rights under the DPDP Act, 2023 (India)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to access — you can view all your data within the App at any time (My People, Upcoming, Wishlists, Settings).
  • Right to correction — all data is editable directly within the App.
  • Right to erasure — you can delete individual entries or all your data at any time via Settings → Delete all my data.
  • Right to withdraw consent — you may revoke notification, contacts, or calendar permission at any time via your device settings. You may also delete all your data from Settings, which effectively ends all data processing.
  • Right to data portability — you can export your data as a JSON file via Settings → Export my data.
  • Right to grievance redressal — you may raise any data-related concern with our Grievance Officer (see Section 15 below).

Because we do not hold your data on any server, most rights are exercised entirely within your own control through the App.

9. Children's privacy

Gifter is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used the App in a way that creates a privacy concern, please contact us at gifterappindia@gmail.com.

10. Microphone access

Gifter requests microphone permission (RECORD_AUDIO on Android) to enable voice input on quiz text fields. When you tap the microphone button on your device keyboard, the system processes your speech locally or via the keyboard's own speech engine. Gifter itself does not record, capture, or transmit any audio. You may decline microphone access without affecting any other App features.

11. Data security

Your data is stored in your device's sandboxed local storage, protected by your device's own security model (screen lock, encryption-at-rest where enabled). Data transmitted to our Cloudflare Worker proxy and Anthropic's API is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS 1.2 or higher. We do not store API responses or your quiz inputs on any server.

12. Data retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the App installed. We do not retain copies of your personal data on any external server. Deleting the App, or using Settings → Delete all my data, permanently removes all your Gifter data from your device.

13. International users

Gifter is designed for users in India and is governed by Indian law. If you access the App from outside India, you do so at your own discretion and are responsible for compliance with local laws. The anonymised AI query described in Section 5a may be processed on servers located outside India (Anthropic operates in the United States). By using the App, you consent to this limited, de-identified data transfer.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last updated.
